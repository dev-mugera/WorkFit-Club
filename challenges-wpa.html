Mountain Climbers Challenge

October 1-31, 2025
3,000 reps per team

Mountain climbers are a dynamic full-body exercise that targets your core, shoulders, and legs while providing cardiovascular benefits. This challenge will help improve your endurance, core strength, and overall fitness level.

Challenge Details

  • Target: 3,000 mountain climbers per furnace team
  • Duration: Entire month of October
  • Tracking: Log your daily reps in the WorkFit app
  • Team Goal: Complete 100 reps per team member daily
  • Rewards: Top performing furnace gets featured in newsletter

How to Do Mountain Climbers

1

Starting Position

Begin in a high plank position with hands under shoulders and body straight.

2

Engage Core

Keep your core tight throughout to maintain proper form.

3

Alternate Legs

Drive one knee toward chest, then quickly switch in a running motion.

4

Maintain Pace

Breathe steadily and maintain a controlled, consistent pace.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Allowing hips to sag or rise too high
  • Placing hands too far forward
  • Holding your breath during the exercise
  • Moving too quickly before mastering form

Plank Hold Challenge

October 1-31, 2025
60 minutes total per team

The plank is one of the most effective core exercises that also engages your shoulders, back, and glutes. This challenge will help build incredible core strength and stability.

Challenge Details

  • Target: 60 minutes of total plank time per furnace team
  • Duration: Entire month of October
  • Tracking: Log your daily plank time in the WorkFit app
  • Team Goal: Complete 2 minutes per team member daily
  • Rewards: Special core strength badge for all participants

How to Do Planks Correctly

1

Forearm Position

Place forearms on ground with elbows aligned below shoulders.

2

Body Alignment

Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

3

Engage Muscles

Tighten your core and glutes to prevent hips from sagging.

4

Breathing

Breathe deeply and steadily throughout the hold.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Letting hips sag toward the floor
  • Raising hips too high
  • Looking up instead of down at hands
  • Holding your breath during the exercise

Step Up Challenge

October 1-31, 2025
3,000 steps daily per team

This step challenge encourages consistent daily movement and can be achieved through various activities like walking, running, or other cardio exercises. It's perfect for building sustainable fitness habits.

Challenge Details

  • Target: 3,000 steps daily per furnace team
  • Duration: Entire month of October
  • Tracking: Use fitness tracker or smartphone step counter
  • Team Goal: Complete 100 steps per team member daily
  • Rewards: Most consistent team wins a group fitness session

How to Maximize Your Steps

1

Daily Walking

Take brisk walks during breaks or after meals.

2

Use Stairs

Opt for stairs instead of elevators when possible.

3

Park Further

Park farther from destinations to add extra steps.

4

Walking Meetings

Take walking meetings when possible.

Tips for Success

  • Break steps into smaller chunks throughout the day
  • Set reminders to get up and move every hour
  • Combine steps with other daily activities
  • Track progress using fitness apps or devices
