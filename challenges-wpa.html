Challenge Details

  • Target: Read "The 5AM Club" and implement the principles

  • Community: Daily check-ins and weekly discussion groups

The 20/20/20 Formula

1

5:00-5:20 AM: Move

20 minutes of intense exercise to boost energy and vitality.

2

5:20-5:40 AM: Reflect

20 minutes of meditation, journaling, or visualization.

3

5:40-6:00 AM: Grow

20 minutes of learning through reading, podcasts, or skill development.

Benefits of Joining

  • Increased productivity and focus throughout the day
  • Better mental clarity and reduced stress
  • Time for personal growth before daily responsibilities
  • Community accountability and support

No Sugar November Challenge

November 1-30, 2025
30 days without added sugar

Reset your taste buds, improve your health, and break free from sugar addiction. This 30-day challenge will help you eliminate added sugars from your diet while providing daily support, recipes, and community encouragement.

Challenge Details

  • Target: 30 days without added sugars
  • Duration: Entire month of November
  • Allowed: Natural sugars from whole fruits
  • Support: Daily recipes, meal plans, and community chat
  • Rewards: Healthier eating habits and improved energy levels

Getting Started Guide

1

Kitchen Cleanout

Remove all sugary snacks, drinks, and processed foods from your home.

2

Meal Planning

Plan your meals for the week to avoid impulsive sugary choices.

3

Healthy Alternatives

Stock up on fruits, nuts, and healthy snacks for cravings.

4

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help manage cravings and stay full.

Common Sugar Sources to Avoid

  • Sodas, juices, and sweetened beverages
  • Candy, cookies, and baked goods
  • Processed foods and sauces
  • Breakfast cereals and flavored yogurts

30-Day Fitness Streak Challenge

November 1-30, 2025
30 consecutive days of activity

Build unstoppable momentum and make fitness a non-negotiable part of your daily routine. This challenge focuses on consistency - any movement counts! From intense workouts to gentle walks, the goal is to move every single day.

Challenge Details

  • Target: 30 consecutive days of physical activity
  • Duration: Entire month of November
  • Minimum: 15 minutes of intentional movement daily
  • Variety: Mix of strength, cardio, flexibility, and active recovery
  • Rewards: Streak badges and consistency certificate

Building Your Streak

1

Set a Schedule

Plan your workout time in advance to make it non-negotiable.

2

Mix It Up

Alternate between different types of workouts to prevent boredom.

3

Active Recovery

Include light activities like walking or stretching on rest days.

4

Track Progress

Use the WorkFit app to log your daily activity and maintain motivation.

Activity Ideas for Busy Days

  • 15-minute bodyweight workout
  • Brisk walk during lunch break
  • Evening stretching or yoga session
  • Active house cleaning or gardening
