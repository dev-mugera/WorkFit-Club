Annual Fitness Challenge 2023

Our biggest community event of the year brought together over 200 participants for a day of fitness, fun, and friendly competition. The Annual Fitness Challenge featured multiple workout stations, team activities, and individual challenges designed to test strength, endurance, and agility.

The event raised over $5,000 for local youth sports programs and created lasting memories for all participants. We're already planning for next year's event, which promises to be even bigger and better!

Special thanks to our sponsors, volunteers, and of course, all the amazing participants who made this event such a tremendous success.

Morning Group Workout Session

Our popular morning group workout sessions continue to draw enthusiastic participants eager to start their day with energy and purpose. This session focused on full-body conditioning with a mix of strength training, cardio intervals, and core work.

Group workouts provide not just physical benefits but also create a supportive community atmosphere where members motivate each other to push through challenges and celebrate achievements together.

New sessions are available every weekday at 6:00 AM and 7:30 AM. All fitness levels are welcome, with modifications provided for every exercise.
