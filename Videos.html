Full Body Workout for Beginners

This complete 30-minute full body workout is perfect for those just starting their fitness journey. No equipment needed - just your body weight and determination!

Workout Structure

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of dynamic stretching and light cardio
  • Circuit 1: Lower body focus (squats, lunges, glute bridges)
  • Circuit 2: Upper body focus (push-ups, tricep dips, plank variations)
  • Circuit 3: Core focus (crunches, leg raises, Russian twists)
  • Cool-down: 5 minutes of static stretching

Modifications Available

Throughout the workout, we provide easier modifications for each exercise. Don't worry if you can't complete all the reps - just do what you can and build from there!

What You'll Need

  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Water bottle
  • Exercise mat (optional but recommended)
  • Positive attitude!

20-Minute HIIT Fat Burner

This high-intensity interval training session is designed to maximize calorie burn in minimal time. Perfect for busy schedules when you need an efficient workout.

Workout Structure

  • Format: 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
  • Rounds: 4 rounds of 5 exercises each
  • Total exercises: 5 (repeated for 4 rounds)
  • Intensity: High - push yourself during work periods

Exercises Included

  • Burpees
  • Mountain climbers
  • High knees
  • Jump squats
  • Plank jacks

Afterburn Effect

HIIT workouts create an "afterburn effect" where your body continues to burn calories at an elevated rate for hours after your workout is complete.
