WorkFit Fun Run

November 1, 2025
7:30 AM - 11:00 AM
Nairobi Jaffery's Sports Club

The WorkFit Fun Run is an exciting community event bringing together runners of all levels for a morning of fitness and fun. Our goal is to create a lively and encouraging environment where everyone can participate, challenge themselves, and enjoy the spirit of a shared athletic experience.

Event Details

  • Distance: 10km main route with 5km option available
  • Registration: Free for all WorkFit members
  • Age Groups: Open to participants aged 16 and above
  • What to Bring: Comfortable running shoes, water bottle, and positive attitude!
  • Amenities: Water stations, first aid, and post-run refreshments provided

Nutrition Workshop

November 15, 2025
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
WorkFit Main Club

Our Nutrition Workshop is designed to help you understand the fundamentals of proper nutrition for fitness and recovery. Led by certified nutrition experts, this interactive session includes cooking demonstrations, meal planning tips, and personalized advice to help you fuel your body effectively for your fitness goals.

Workshop Topics

  • Macronutrients 101: Understanding proteins, carbs, and fats for fitness
  • Pre and Post-Workout Nutrition: What to eat and when for optimal performance
  • Meal Planning Strategies: Creating balanced meals for busy schedules
  • Hydration Essentials: The importance of proper hydration for performance
  • Supplements Guide: What works and what doesn't - evidence-based approach

What to Expect

  • Interactive cooking demonstrations with tasting samples
  • Personalized nutrition assessments
  • Sample meal plans and recipes to take home
  • Q&A session with certified nutrition experts
  • Comprehensive take-home materials and resources

Team Building Day

November 22, 2025
8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Karura Forest

Our Team Building Day is designed to strengthen bonds between furnace members through a series of fun outdoor activities and challenges. Set in the beautiful Karura Forest, this event will include team challenges, problem-solving activities, and opportunities to connect with your furnace teammates in a relaxed, natural setting.

Activities Include

  • Team Challenges: Collaborative problem-solving activities designed to build trust and communication
  • Nature Walk: Guided walk through the scenic trails of Karura Forest
  • Fitness Games: Fun, active games that promote teamwork and healthy competition
  • Communication Exercises: Activities specifically designed to improve team communication
  • Group Picnic: Relaxed lunch and social time to connect with teammates

What to Bring

  • Comfortable outdoor clothing and sturdy shoes

  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
