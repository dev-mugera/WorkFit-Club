How to Install WorkFit App

1 Open WorkFit in Your Browser

Go to the WorkFit website using your preferred browser

2 Look for the Install Button

Depending on your device and browser, look for one of these options:

Chrome Desktop

Look for the install icon in the address bar on the right side

Android Chrome

Follow the prompts to add WorkFit to your home screen. The app will be installed and ready to use!

4 Launch the App

Find the WorkFit icon on your home screen and tap to open the app anytime
